Workout Tracker

This Android application will catalog two forms of workouts: Weight Training and Cardiovascular Exercise.

Weight Training

This option will allow you to track your lifting repetitions and sets by organizing them based on the kind of lifting technique (i.e. curls, benchpress) you are doing. You will be able to pick from a pre-set list of techniques or have the option of creating your own if you can't find yours. Furthermore, the app will compare your current workout to previous ones to determine if you are improving or not.

Cardiovascular

This option will allow you to track your endurance and calorie consumption based on a pre-set list of cardiovascular exercises including jogging, biking, walking and elliptical. Endurance improvement will be based on duration and intensity of the exercise.
